About Me

Ph.D. candidate in ECE with a strong foundation in vehicle dynamics, control systems, and embedded software development. I build scalable autonomous vehicle platforms to study functional safety, cybersecurity, and real-time system behavior. My experience spans torque vectoring, steer-by-wire systems, RTOS-based motor control, and hardware-in-the-loop validation. I aim to pursue a career in functional safety and systems engineering, developing fail-safe and fail-operational architectures for next-generation electric and autonomous vehicles.
